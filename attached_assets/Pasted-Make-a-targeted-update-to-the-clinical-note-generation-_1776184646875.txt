Required workflow for note content generation:
Transcript/audio -> structured clinical extraction -> SOAP note generation

- symptoms discussed
- current medications
- supplements
- hormone therapies
- past medical history
- surgical history if mentioned
- prior treatments or medication trials
- side effects
- allergies if mentioned
- lifestyle factors relevant to care
- mental health history
- secondary concerns discussed during the visit

Then generate the SOAP note from BOTH:
1. the transcript

- be more thorough
